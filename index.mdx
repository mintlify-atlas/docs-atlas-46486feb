## Why GitAgent?

Every AI framework has its own structure. There is no universal, portable way to define an agent that works across Claude Code, OpenAI, LangChain, CrewAI, and AutoGen. GitAgent fixes that.

- **Git-native** — Version control, branching, diffing, and collaboration are built in
- **Framework-agnostic** — Define once, export to any framework with adapters
- **Compliance-ready** — FINRA, Federal Reserve, SEC, and CFPB support baked into the manifest
- **Composable** — Agents can extend parents, declare dependencies, and delegate to sub-agents

See the [introduction](/introduction) for a full overview.
